Understanding Macronutrients

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are your body's main source of energy. They can be found in foods such as bread, pasta, rice, fruits, and vegetables. It's important to choose whole grain options for a healthier diet.

Proteins

Proteins are essential for building and repairing tissues. Good sources of protein include meat, fish, eggs, dairy products, legumes, and nuts. Including a variety of protein sources in your diet is crucial.

Fats

Fats are necessary for your body to function properly. They help in the absorption of vitamins and provide energy. Healthy fats can be found in avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil. It's important to limit saturated and trans fats.
